Welcome to Lexorva – Let’s Get You Set Up

Step 1 – Purchase a Plan

Visit the Pricing page and choose the plan that fits your needs. Securely check out via Stripe.

Step 2 – Get Your Promo Code

After purchase, you’ll receive a unique promo code via email. This unlocks full Lexorva access for the number of users allowed by your plan.

Step 3 – Sign Up & Login

Go to the Sign Up/Login page. Enter your promo code to activate your account with full access — no credit card needed.

Step 4 – Upload a Document

Inside Lexorva, click the “Upload” icon to submit a PDF, legal document, or image. Lexorva will analyze it in real time using GPT-4 logic.

Step 5 – Ask Legal Questions

Type your legal question clearly. Lexorva will understand your document + your question, and deliver a smart, strategic response in seconds.

Step 6 – Download Your Strategy Report

After Lexorva finishes responding, click the “Download Report” button beneath the answer to save a clean, professional PDF for your records or courtroom use.
