Welcome to Lexorva – Let’s Get You Fully Set Up

Step 1 – Choose and Purchase Your Plan

Begin by visiting our Pricing page. You'll see all available Lexorva plans, including:

• Individual Plan – $74.99/month for 1 user
• Firm Plans – From 10 to 200+ users

Click “Select Plan” under the option that fits your needs. You’ll be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page where you can enter your payment details. Once payment is complete, Stripe will confirm your subscription.

Step 2 – Receive Your Promo Code

After subscribing, you will automatically receive a unique Lexorva promo code by email.

• Individual Users: This code is tied to your personal account. Do not share it.
• Firms: This code allows the number of users included in your plan to sign up without entering payment — just the code.

If you don’t receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

Step 3 – Share the Promo Code with Your Team (Firms Only)

If you're managing a firm plan, distribute your promo code to your colleagues. Each team member should:

1. Visit the Sign Up/Login page.
2. Enter their name, email, and create a password.
3. Input your shared promo code when prompted.
4. They will gain instant access without needing a credit card.

Step 4 – Sign Up & Activate Your Account

Whether you’re an individual or a firm user, go to the Sign Up/Login page. Fill in your details and paste your promo code.

Lexorva will verify your code, activate your access, and unlock all features instantly — including legal strategy generation, document review, evidence analysis, and more.

Step 5 – Start Using Lexorva

Once logged in, you can:

• Ask any legal question in plain language
• Upload a case file, PDF, or document for review
• Generate advanced legal strategy reports
• Analyze opposing counsel’s arguments
• Download results and reports instantly

Every tool is designed to be intuitive and require no training — just enter your issue and Lexorva does the rest.

Need Support or Help?

If you run into any issues:

• Contact us through the Contact Page
• Or email our support team directly

We’ll get back to you quickly and make sure you’re fully onboarded without hassle.
